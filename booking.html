About This Experience

Experience the vibrant nightlife of Nairobi with a local guide who knows the best spots. Visit hidden gems, popular clubs, and enjoy live music at local rates. This 5-hour tour includes entry to 3 venues, one welcome drink at each location, and insider access to the real Nairobi after dark.

Experience Highlights

  • Visit 3 authentic local nightlife venues
  • Enjoy live music from local artists
  • Skip tourist prices with local rates
  • Learn about Nairobi's music and club culture
  • Meet locals and make new friends

Entry fees to all venues
